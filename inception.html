Inception (2010)

Director: Christopher Nolan

Cast: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page

“Inception” is a sci-fi thriller that explores the boundaries of dreams and reality. Dom Cobb is a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can perform an impossible task: inception — planting an idea in someone’s mind.
