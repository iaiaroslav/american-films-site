Pulp Fiction (1994)

Director: Quentin Tarantino

Cast: John Travolta, Uma Thurman, Samuel L. Jackson

“Pulp Fiction” is a masterpiece of non-linear storytelling that intertwines multiple stories of crime, redemption, and dark humor. Known for its sharp dialogue, iconic scenes, and unforgettable soundtrack, it redefined modern American cinema.
